BTE Project

In the summer of 2019, a YouTuber named FTSPippen uploaded a video calling all
Minecraft players around the world to come together to create a 1:1 scale
representation of the world in Minecraft. This is by far the largest community
project ever undertaken in the games history......and I'm a part of it.


So when I was in sixth year, a few months before the project was announced
I had a taken a keen interest in the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant disaster that
took place in April of 1986 in Ukraine due to my study of nuclear physics in my
physics class. A few months later, Pippen put the word out and I knew what
I was going to do


I trawled through the subreddit for the project, only to find to my delight that
no one had claimed the rights to build the Power Plant. I, along with a good friend, set
to work. Over the following weeks, many enthusiastic builders joined our endavour to recreate
the once great power plant in Minecraft. At the same time, the project as a whole was taking
the gaming community of the internet by storm. At the time of writing, the Discord server that
is the main hub for the project has a total of 240,000 members


So far, we have done an incredible job in doing our part of the project to the best of our ability.
We will continue to carry out this monumental task in the hopes that one day, no matter how much
the world around us changes, people can still go back to this project to see what the world looked like
back then and have the chance to walk around in it.
