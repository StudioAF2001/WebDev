My Favourite Hobby

My favourite hobby is to build and resarch PC's and their parts
I'm currently in the process of designing and building my own personal
desktop PC so I will tell you a bit more about that.


In my home-designed, home-built PC, I have chosen then AMD Ryzen 5 3600
as my processor. At the time of writing this, it is one of te best price for
performance processor on the market for gaming as well as just overall usage.


For my motherboard, I have chosen an ASUS ROG Strix b450f gaming. It provides
good performance and upgradability for a decent price, along well a good I/O.
It will allow me to upgrade my BIOS to allow for an upgrade in CPU to the Ryzen Zen 3
platform. and best of all, it has RGB!!


I'll keep the RAM specs short and to the point. I have purchased 16GB of 3600MHz
DDR4 Corsair Vengeance RGB. The high speed will be perfect for the AMD architecture
for extremely fast communication between the RAM and the CPU.


For my case, I have chosen the Corsair 4000d airflow. It is an mid-ATX case that has
good airflow from the corrugated front panel and the tempered glass side panel prodes
a look inside the machine. It has a decent front I/O, plenty sufficient for daily use.
